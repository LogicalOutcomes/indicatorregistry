{% block title %}Export{% endblock %}

Indicators can be exported from the LogicalOutcomes financial literacy indicator registry in two ways. When viewing a single indicator click the "Export Indicator" button to download a PDF copy of the indicator. When browsing the registry, multiple indicators can be downloaded by selecting them on the right and clicking export. For more information see the help page on downloading content. In future phases, we plan to offer exports into csv, Excel, DHIS2 and possibly XLSForms. For more information, contact us.
